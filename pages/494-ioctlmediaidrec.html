IoctlMediaIdRec

 This structure is used to uniquely identify the medium in a drive.  It is
 used in the following IOCTL fns:
    440dH 46H (set media ID)
    440dH 66H (query media ID)

    DvRq 13H  (generic IOCTL for block devices)
