                          xText

This is the hypertext authoring system used to create the
TECH Help! database that you are viewing right now.

 ■ Create the text with your own favorite word processor
   or text editor.  Insert simple "dot commands" to
   identify topics and connect hyperlinks.

 ■ Useful for any type of on-screen data, including
   context-sensitive help for application programs.

 ■ No fees or royalties for distributing the xText Viewer.
   It is available in eight international languages.
   Currently in use at over 30 million sites.
