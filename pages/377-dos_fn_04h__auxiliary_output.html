DOS Fn 04H: Auxiliary Output

                                                         Compatibility: 1.0+
 Expects: AH    04H
          DL    character to send to the Standard Auxiliary Device
          ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Returns: none
          ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    Info: Sends the character in DL to the Standard Auxiliary Device, COM1
          or AUX.
