INT 2fH 1682H: End Critical Section

                                                      Compatibility: Win 3.x
 Expects: AX    1682H
          ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Returns: none
          ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    Info: This releases control, allowing Windows (or other VM handler) to
          resume time-slice sharing among currently-active tasks.

          After signalling entry into a critical section (INT 2fH 1681H),
          you should use this service as soon as possible.
